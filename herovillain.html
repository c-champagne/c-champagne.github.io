Hero vs. Villain

Created March 2020
Wouldn't it be great to answer all of those "who would win" hypotheticals?

Hero vs. Villian lets you run your own personal battle involving any two things you can imagine. It is as simple as entering each fighter's name, their weapon, and then clicking each fighter's "attack" button. And while the game is called "Hero vs. Villain", your choices are only limited by your imagination. So go ahead and have Batman fight Spongebob Squarepants. Or maybe pit Thanos against a bucket of water. Show off the winner to your friends! And whether you choose to fight fairly (alternating turns) or you clearly favor one side, I won't tell. The fight is up to you!
