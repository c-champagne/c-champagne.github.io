Who is Cassie?

A

Let's answer some common questions.


What's your background?

I am a former educator turned tech wizard.
My craving for knowledge and an innate desire to help others led me to pursue an English and Education degree. Some of my early roles include:

  • Teaching English (first to high school students in Massachusetts, later to Chinese elementary students, virtually)
  • Spending one-on-one time with children with special needs in a beautiful mountain setting
  • Serving in two different administrative roles within two college campuses

I made the decision to move into the tech industry and completed a six month long program which taught me front end and back end developer skills. I began working at PagerDuty—starting out as a member of the technical support team before transitioning to a technical writer.


Why tech?

Technology has always held a special place in my heart. My early introduction to computers and the internet gave me a familiarity with technology that always caught the attention of my friends, family and coworkers. My past is sprinkled with moments where I assisted teachers with starting movie projectors, helped co-workers fix the meeting room's audio for Zoom calls or set-up an absurd number of printers and Xboxes for college students.


Technology is advancing faster than ever and has become inextricably interwoven into our daily lives. What better way to assist others than by helping people understand that technology?


What do you do outside of work?

I enjoy spending time in nature—hiking, birdwatching and photography balance out my time spent in front of screens. I also love to bake, read and try my hand at various creative projects.

Still have questions? Don't hesitate to reach out, I would love to chat!
